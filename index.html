ABOUT

Disco Centric are a wedding and function band based in Cardiff, South Wales. As one of the freshest, innovative function bands on the circuit, we play a range of classic disco and funk, synth-driven electronica, as well as contemporary floor fillers.
Available to play weddings, corporate events, parties and more throughout Cardiff, Swansea, Bristol & the South West, Midlands, Manchester, London and beyond!

We offer a range of packages for your wedding or event. Whether you'd like the standard 5-piece (vocals, guitar, bass, keys, drums) or the showstopping 8-piece (with additional horn section), there's something to get that dancefloor FULL!
