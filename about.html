ABOUT

Disco Centric are one of the freshest, innovative Welsh function bands on the circuit. Along with classic disco and funk songs, we play electronic songs (entirely live!), pop and contemporary floor fillers. Displaying excellent musicianship in an energy-filled live performance, our band will have the crowd singing and spinning until the lights come on! Available to play weddings, corporate events, parties and more throughoutÂ Cardiff, Swansea, Bristol & the South West, Midlands, Manchester, London and beyond!

We offer a DJ service (before and in between sets) with every booking if you need it and you can choose the songs that fill this playlist with a mixture of the thousands we already have. All members are highly trained, full-time professional musicians who enjoy nothing more than entertaining great audiences!

If you're looking for a wedding band, a function band or a party band, let Disco Centric do all of the work for you. We offer packages in three formats - 5-piece band (vocals, guitar, bass, keyboards, drums), 7-piece band (with additional trumpet & trombone) and an 8-piece band (with everything listed as well as a saxophonist). Rest assured with every option, we are fully committed to all shows that we play and are fully passionate about performing live.

With our repertoire including dance hits from the 60's and 70's (Chic, Stevie Wonder, ABBA) all the way up to the 2000's and 2010's (Bruno Mars, OutKast, Beyonce) there's something for everyone! All our songs have been rehearsed to a very high standard and have been worked on meticulously to include crowd singalongs, participation and even some mash ups of your favorite tunes. Still not convinced? Depending on which package you purchase we can include up to 3 songs of your choice! No more deciding on which song to be added to the set list for your first dance, you could select one each for the bride and groom and one crowd pleaser for your friends.

Disco Centric are fully insured and PAT tested - you are welcome to view our certifications by request. Any further questions or interested in booking us for an event? Please contact us on our contact form and we'll be happy to look at what we can do for your event. We aim to respond to all enquirers within 24 hours and look forward to hearing from you!
